Online Quiz Competition for the Celebration of 5th Anniversary of NEP 2020






The Human Resource Development Centre (HRDC) and the Institute’s Innovation Council (IIC) of Delhi Technological University (DTU) jointly organized an Online Quiz Competition on July 29, 2025, to celebrate the 5th anniversary of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. The event aimed to enhance awareness and understanding of NEP 2020 among participants through an engaging and interactive platform.

The quiz, conducted via Google Forms, received 109 registrations from across the university community. A total of 48 participants attempted the quiz, which comprised 20 multiple-choice questions based on key aspects of NEP 2020. The competition provided an opportunity for participants to test their knowledge and reflect on the policy’s vision and implementation.The performance was commendable, with eight participants securing the highest score of 19 out of 20. The average score recorded was 15.85, while the median score stood at 17. E-certificates were awarded to all participants who successfully attempted the quiz, recognizing their enthusiasm and participation.

Three-Week Technical Training Programme for DANICS Probationers(60th, 61st and 62nd Batches)







The Human Resource Development Centre (HRDC) of Delhi Technological University (DTU), in collaboration with the Directorate of Training (UT Civil Services), Government of NCT of Delhi, organized a three-week Technical Training Programme for DANICS probationers of the 60th, 61st, and 62nd batches from 26th May to 13th June 2025. The programme aimed to enhance participants’ technical and administrative skills through expert sessions, interactive discussions, and practical exposure.

The inaugural function featured addresses by senior DTU officials and a keynote by Shri Rajneesh Gupta, IPS, Joint Commissioner of Police, IFSO, Special Cell, Delhi Police, who emphasized the growing importance of cyber awareness and technological readiness in governance.The training included 34 sessions by experts from institutions like IIIT Delhi, JNU, MNNIT Allahabad, HCL Tech, and the Ministry of Education, covering Artificial Intelligence, IoT, Cybersecurity, Robotics, and Data-Driven Governance. Field visits to RailTel and CERT-In offered hands-on insights into digital infrastructure and cyber resilience.
